# Legal and licensing boundaries

Data is published only when an authority supplies structured geometry and redistribution is permitted. Cache permission and redistribution permission are separate decisions.

- DIPUL WFS geometry remains unmodified and live under CC BY-ND 4.0.
- French Géoportail geometry is queried live and not bundled.
- Finland and Netherlands snapshots follow their recorded CC BY 4.0 and CC0 terms.
- Sweden's LFV files remain unmodified; renderer filters do not rewrite the source.
- Norway remains a direct handoff because Avinor prohibits republication.
- Italy remains an authenticated handoff; personal exports stay ignored because d-flight reserves public redistribution.
- Canada excludes the protected NAV CANADA-derived database.

An unrecorded licence means “do not redistribute,” not implied permission.
