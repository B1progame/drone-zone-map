# Zone explanation model

Point checks keep overlapping zones separate and order them by operational severity. A result should show the strongest available official name, category, description, altitude, validity, legal reference, authority/contact, source, update time, and verification link.

When official text is absent, the UI uses a clearly factual fallback such as the official layer and restriction classification; it never invents legal permission. Empty results say that no restriction was found in the downloaded or queried official dataset and remind the pilot that temporary restrictions and NOTAMs may be missing.
