# Aeris wiki

![Aeris homepage](../public/screenshots/home.jpg)

Aeris is a privacy-first drone airspace and weather planning workspace. It is built for the responsible moment before takeoff: find a place, read the official context, compare conditions, plan a route, and keep useful map context available offline.
